Google Arts & Culture allow users to access content of over 2000 museums around the world. Among all the archives, collections and other offered features, there is also more than 300 virtual reality guided tours waiting for you to be taken.

What about visiting ancient Greek temples? Or exploring the masterpieces in England’s first purpose-built public art gallery? You can even get to know one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, Christ the Redeemer in Brazil.
